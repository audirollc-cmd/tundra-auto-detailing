Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Tundra Auto Detailing, operated by Detailing Dispatch (“we,” “our,” or “us”), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

Personal Data

When you use our contact form or booking system, we may collect personally identifiable information such as your name, phone number, email address, vehicle information, and service address.

Phone & Text Communication

When you call our business number, our AI scheduling agent may collect your phone number and scheduling preferences to send you a booking link via SMS. Standard messaging rates may apply.

Automatically Collected Data

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and the pages you have viewed. This data is used to improve our website and services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule and provide our auto detailing services
  • Send booking confirmations and service reminders via text message
  • Respond to your inquiries and customer service requests
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Communicate with you about promotions or updates (with your consent)

SMS/Text Messaging

By calling our business number, you consent to receiving a booking link via text message. You may opt out of future text messages at any time by replying STOP. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as our scheduling platform (Calendly), provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party services such as Calendly for booking. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some features of our website.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the “Last updated” date.
