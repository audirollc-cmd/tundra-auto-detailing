OUR STORY

Built on Hustle, Driven by Quality

From a single detail to five brands across the Twin Cities — this is who we are.

Tundra Auto Detailing is part of Detailing Dispatch, a growing network of five local detailing brands serving neighborhoods across the Minneapolis/St. Paul metro area. Each brand is hyper-local, built to serve specific communities with the kind of personal attention that big franchises can’t match.

Founded by Mohamed and Abdirahim, Detailing Dispatch was created with a simple mission: deliver premium, mobile auto detailing with the easiest booking experience in the industry. We saw an opportunity to combine cutting-edge technology with hands-on craftsmanship.

When you call Tundra Auto Detailing, you’re connected to our AI-powered scheduling agent that speaks both English and Spanish. Within seconds, you receive a booking link via text message — no hold times, no back-and-forth, no apps to download. Just seamless scheduling that respects your time.

Our detailers are trained professionals who take pride in every vehicle they touch. We use only professional-grade products and follow meticulous processes to ensure every car leaves with a showroom-quality finish. From a quick standard detail to a full restoration-level treatment, we bring the shop to your driveway.
